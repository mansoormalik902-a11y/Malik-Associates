About Malik Associates

Malik Associates, Chartered Accountants & CMAs, is a professional accounting, taxation, audit and business advisory firm dedicated to delivering practical, value-driven solutions to businesses across Pakistan, the UAE and international markets.

With a team of experienced Chartered Accountants, CMAs, CPAs and financial professionals, we provide expertise in financial reporting, taxation, audit & assurance, corporate compliance, virtual CFO services, corporate finance and business consultancy.

Our commitment to excellence, integrity and client success enables us to build long-term partnerships while helping organizations achieve sustainable growth, regulatory compliance and operational efficiency.

Who We Are

Malik Associates is a premier accounting, taxation, audit and business advisory firm committed to delivering practical financial solutions tailored to modern business requirements.

Our team of qualified professionals combines technical expertise, industry knowledge and strategic insight to help organizations strengthen financial performance and maintain regulatory compliance.

Serving clients across Pakistan and the UAE, we support startups, SMEs and established enterprises in achieving sustainable growth and long-term success.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading professional services firm known for excellence, integrity and innovation in accounting, taxation, audit and advisory services.

We aspire to become the trusted partner of choice for businesses seeking strategic guidance, financial leadership and sustainable value creation.

Through continuous improvement and professional excellence, we aim to set new standards in client service and business advisory.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable financial solutions, strategic advisory services and modern reporting systems that empower informed business decision-making.

We are committed to providing timely, accurate and value-driven services that enhance operational efficiency, financial transparency and regulatory compliance.

By building long-term relationships founded on trust, professionalism and integrity, we help businesses achieve their growth objectives with confidence.
